Crimes in Chicago(2012-2016)

This stacked multi-line graph presents user the crime pattern throughout a year, and also allows user to compare crime counts among different years. Mouseover the lines triggers tooltip shows exact crime number of the particular month. The legend under x-axis indicates color encoding of a year.

The chart shows that in all years, summer time has more frequent crime occurences. Interestingly, the overall crime declined over years but sames stopped at 2016.
